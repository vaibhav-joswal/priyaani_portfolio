Lumina App

UI/UX Design — 2023

Role

Lead Product Designer

Timeline

4 Months (Q2 - Q3)

Tools

Figma, Webflow, After Effects

Overview

Lumina is a next-generation productivity suite designed for creative professionals who find traditional project management tools too rigid. The goal was to create a fluid, intuitive interface that adapts to the user's workflow rather than forcing them into a predefined structure.

Problem Statement

Users reported feeling overwhelmed by complex dashboards and cluttered interfaces. The primary challenge was to reduce cognitive load while maintaining powerful functionality for deep-work sessions and collaborative brainstorming.

The Process

From initial conceptualization to high-fidelity implementation, our journey focused on user-centric iterations and continuous feedback.

edit_note

Wireframing

We started with low-fidelity sketches to map out the core navigation. The focus was on "Spatial Organization" - allowing users to move cards in a 3D-like workspace.

palette

Visual Identity

The visual language uses glassmorphism and soft glows to represent "Ideas in Focus". We chose a dark-themed palette to reduce eye strain during long working hours.
