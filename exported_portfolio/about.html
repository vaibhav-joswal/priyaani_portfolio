About Priyani

Design Student & Creative Thinker based in Jaipur.

Currently a 3rd-year Bachelor of Design (B.Des) student at JKLU Jaipur, I am deeply invested in the intersection of human psychology and digital interfaces. My work revolves around creating impactful, human-centric solutions that bridge the gap between complex functionality and aesthetic simplicity.

My design philosophy is rooted in the pursuit of meaningful experiences. I believe every pixel should serve a purpose and every interaction should feel intuitive, telling a story that resonates with the user long after the screen is turned off.

Designing with intent, creating with soul.
